Our Story

A café built on slow mornings & honest craft

Honey & Haze was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee should come with a moment of stillness. We source single-origin beans, use natural honey in place of refined sugar, and bake everything fresh each morning.

Our space is intentional: warm lighting, natural textures, and no rush. Whether you're here to work, to meet someone, or simply to breathe — you belong here.
